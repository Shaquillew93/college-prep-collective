FAQ

Common questions.

When should we start working together? +
The earlier the better — sophomore year is ideal. But I've helped seniors with months until deadlines get great results too. If you're reading this, it's not too late.
Do you only work with high-achieving students? +
Absolutely not. I work with students across all academic profiles. The goal is finding the right fit college — not the most prestigious one.
What makes you different from a school counselor? +
School counselors are incredible — and often stretched thin with hundreds of students. As a private counselor, I give your student my full attention and expertise, tailored entirely to them.
Do you help with financial aid? +
Yes. Financial aid strategy is woven into everything I do. I help families understand what colleges actually cost, how to appeal award letters, and how to find scholarship funding.
Are sessions virtual or in-person? +
All sessions are virtual, which means I can work with families anywhere in the country.
What if my student is already a senior? +
Senior year is one of the most impactful times to get support. Essay coaching, application review, deadline management, and financial aid strategy can all make a significant difference even late in the process.
